About Us

Aurora Hotel Plaza

Aurora Hotel, located in Bien Hoa, Dong Nai, is perfectly sit uated for those who want to explore the city. The hotel's convenient location provides guests with easy access to all the exciting attractions in the lively city. In addition to its prime location, Aurora Hotel boasts a variety of top-class facilities to enhance your stay in Bien Hoa, including free Wi-Fi, 24-hour front desk and room service, facilities for disabled guests, and luggage storage.

With 66 cozy rooms equipped with a range of amenities such as complimentary wirele ss internet, air conditioning, and wake-up service, guests can escape the stresses of the day . The hotel also offers a hot tub, fitness center, outdoor pool, and kids' pool, providing guests w ith a relaxing atmosphere. Aurora Hotel is an ideal choice for travelers who are looking for comfort, convenience, and charm during their stay in Bien Hoa, Dong Nai.
